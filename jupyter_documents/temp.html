ABOUTCLOUD SPEED

CLOUD SPEED was established in Nov 2018, headquartered in Taiwan. It is committed to providing cloud-based next-generation Web security solutions for its customers and partners from the United States, China, Japan, South Korea, Israel and so on.

With the rapid development of the Internet worldwide, especially the advent of the mobile Internet era, cloud computing has become a public service anytime and anywhere, and the network quality and security of these services have become the decisive factors to ensure the continuous operation of cloud computing.

At present, CLOUD SPEED has deployed several cloud computing centers around the world, and reserves hundreds of G of defense bandwidth. On the premise of ensuring fast visit to Web, under the mode of "zero deployment", "zero maintenance" and "cloud defense", it prevents customs from a series of security threats against Web systems, including XSS, SQL injection, Trojan horse, 0day attack, DDoS botnet, DNS attack, etc.

CLOUD SPEED independently develops big Data analysis technology and simplifies big data processing, making it easy, fast, scalable and cost-effective for you to provision distributed Hadoop clusters and process your data. This helps you to streamline your business through better decisions based on massive data analysis completed in real time.

CLOUD SPEED Cloud provides a comprehensive suite of cloud computing services to businesses worldwide, step-by-step tutorials to help guide you through deploying and hosting projects on CLOUD SPEED Cloud.

In the past, present and future, CLOUD SPEED will continuously improve its service quality and provide first-class service to customers.
